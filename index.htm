Chongzhou Fang

Undergraduate Student of Information Engineering

I am Chongzhou Fang, an undergraduate student in Southeast University, China (SEU).

I entered SEU in September 2016 as an undergraduate student majoring in information engineering. I will graduate in June 2020.

My current research interests include VLSI architecture design and design automation. My current focus lies in high level synthesis and design and optimization of ASIC. I have finished two conference papers on such topics and they have been accepted by IEEE International Conference on ASIC (ASICON).

Previously I was researching on molecular computing. I synthesized a neuron, the basic element of neural networks, in chemical reaction networks (CRNs). I published the results in IEEE SiPS 2018 (see Publications). Further results have been submitted to IEEE Transactions on Nanotechnology.
