PhD Student of Electrical and Computer Engineering

    [chongzhoufang@outlook.com]

I am Chongzhou Fang, a PhD candidate at University of California, Davis. My research interest lies in heterogeneous cloud and edge security. I focus on both scheduler security and side-channel attack and defense in heterogeneous computing resources like FPGAs.

Previously I obtained my bachelor's degree at Southeast University (SEU), China.
